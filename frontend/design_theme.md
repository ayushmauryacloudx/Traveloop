# Traveloop Design System

## Logo
- **Signature Mark**: Globe with an orbit ring + gold satellite dot. 
- **Versions**:
  - **Dark Hero**: For splash/login screens.
  - **Light**: For cards/docs.
  - **Icon-only**: For app icon, favicon, nav bar. (Top-left nav uses a glowing blue globe variant).

## Colors
- **Ocean Deep** (`#071C3F`): Backgrounds, hero sections.
- **Azure Blue** (`#1A6EF5`): Primary buttons, links, accents.
- **Sky Blue** (`#4FA3F7`): Highlights, particles, continent fills, italicized accent text (like \"great adventure\").
- **Journey Gold** (`#F5C842`): Orbit dot, CTAs, brand pop, uppercase subtitles.
- **Mist White** (`#E8F0FE`): Text on dark, light surfaces.
- **Slate Muted** (`#8BA7D4`): Labels, subtitles, placeholders, nav links.
- **Card Gradients**: Deep contextual gradients for destination cards (e.g., blue for Paris, burgundy for Tokyo, green for Bali, brown for Dubai).

## Typography
- **Headings, Wordmark, Hero Text**: Playfair Display (serif). Section headings often use this font with a bold weight. Accent phrases can use italics with a lighter blue gradient.
- **UI Text, Buttons, Forms, Body Copy**: DM Sans (sans-serif).
- **Subtitles/Eyebrow text**: Small, uppercase, wide letter-spacing (e.g., \"INTELLIGENT · ADVENTUROUS · PERSONAL\").

## UI Components & Layouts
- **Backgrounds**: Dark Ocean Deep background with subtle atmospheric glows (e.g., a large subtle planetary sphere glow on the right of the hero) and small floating \"star\" particles.
- **Navigation Bar**: Transparent or dark blend. Logo on left, centered nav links (Slate Muted), primary Azure Blue CTA on right.
- **Buttons**: 
  - *Primary (Azure Blue)*: Bright blue, Mist White text.
  - *Gold CTA*: Solid Journey Gold (`#F5C842`) with dark text, often used with a \"+\" icon (e.g., \"+ Plan a Trip\").
  - *Ghost / Secondary*: Dark fill with a subtle light/blue border and Mist White text (e.g., \"Explore Destinations\").
- **Inputs & Search Bar**: Dark, semi-transparent fill with subtle borders, using Slate Muted for placeholder text and icons. Includes rounded corners.
- **Section Headers**: Playfair Display text, accented with a small, thick vertical Azure Blue line to the left of the text.
- **Destination Cards**: Rounded corners, rich color gradients serving as backgrounds. City names in bold serif (Mist White), country names in small uppercase sans-serif (Slate Muted).
- **Floating Action Buttons**: A pill-shaped Gold CTA fixed to the bottom right of the screen.
